The biggest uncertainty about dental treatment abroad isn't quality or price — it's the question **"How does it actually work, step by step?"**. This guide walks through the whole journey so you know exactly what to expect and how to plan.

## Phase 1: First enquiry (from home)

It all starts **online**, with no travel:
1. You send us an **enquiry in English** describing your concern.
2. Ideally attach a **recent X-ray (OPG/panoramic)** if you have one — so we can assess what's possible in advance.
3. We reply with an initial assessment and any questions.

That gives you clarity before you travel — no trip "into the unknown".

## Phase 2: Treatment plan & quote

Based on your information you receive:
- a **written treatment plan in English** with the planned steps,
- a **quote (fixed price)**,
- the **number of trips needed** and a rough timeline.

Only once the numbers and plan are clear do you book travel. For precise implant planning, a **CBCT (3D X-ray)** is taken on site.

## Phase 3: Travelling to Opole

Opole is easy to reach: ~1 hour from **Wrocław** and **Katowice** airports, with budget flights from the UK and Ireland; right on the A4 motorway (~1.5 h from the German border). We help with **scheduling** so travel and treatment fit together efficiently, and advise on accommodation near the clinic.

## Phase 4: On site — diagnostics & treatment

- **Examination + CBCT** and confirmation/fine-tuning of the plan.
- **Treatment** — depending on the case: implants, [All-on-4](/all-on-4-poland), crowns, root canal, etc.
- Everything **discussed in English** — you understand every step.
- For implants, often **immediate loading** (fixed temporary teeth the same day) where medically sound.

## Phase 5: Healing (at home)

The longest part happens **back home**:
- For implants, the bone heals over **3-6 months** (osseointegration) — you spend that time living normally at home.
- Routine check-ups (e.g. hygiene visits) are done by your **local dentist** — no need to travel for those.

This usually means only **two trips** to Opole (procedure + final restoration).

## Phase 6: Final restoration & aftercare

- Second trip: the **final restoration** (e.g. final bridge/crown after healing).
- **Guarantee & aftercare** are set out in writing; in a guarantee case we correct the work (details: [guarantee & aftercare](/blog/guarantee-and-aftercare-dental-work-poland)).
- We stay **reachable in English**, also after you return home.

## How many trips — typical examples

| Treatment | Trips (typical) |
|---|---|
| Single implant | usually 2 (placement + crown) |
| All-on-4 | 2 (implants + immediate temporaries, later final bridge) |
| Crowns / veneers | often 1-2 |
| Root canal | often 1 |

## Documents & preparation

- **ID/passport** (check current travel rules), book travel early,
- send any existing **X-rays/records** in advance,
- a list of your **medications/medical conditions** (important for planning).

## Conclusion

The process is predictable and transparent: **enquiry with an X-ray → written plan in English → travel & treatment → healing at home → final restoration → aftercare in English.** Usually two trips suffice, and the longest part (healing) happens comfortably at home. That turns high-quality dental care at Polish prices into a manageable, low-stress project.
