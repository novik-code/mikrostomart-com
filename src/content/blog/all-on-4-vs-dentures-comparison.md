With full tooth loss there are two routes: the classic **removable full denture** or fixed teeth on implants (**All-on-4**). The day-to-day difference is enormous. Here's the honest comparison.

## The two options

- **Full denture:** a removable acrylic body with teeth, held by suction/adhesive
- **All-on-4:** a fixed bridge on 4 implants per jaw, screwed in place — not removed

## At-a-glance comparison

| Criterion | Full denture | All-on-4 |
|---|---|---|
| **Hold** | Wobbles, needs adhesive | Firm, no slipping |
| **Chewing power** | 20-40% | 80-100% |
| **Comfort** | Foreign-body feel, palate plate | Like natural teeth |
| **Bone loss** | Continues | Stopped |
| **Taste** | Palate covered | Palate free |
| **Durability** | 5-8 years | 20+ years |
| **Cost (Poland)** | from ~€500-1,500 | from €6,000-9,000 |

## The problems with full dentures

Many wearers know them:
- **Wobbling** when speaking and eating
- **Sore spots** on the gums
- **Adhesive** as a daily necessity
- **Reduced taste** (palate plate in the upper jaw)
- **Ongoing bone loss** → the denture fits worse over time
- **Psychological burden** (fear it might fall out)

## The advantages of All-on-4

- ✅ **Firm hold** — no adhesive, no wobbling
- ✅ **Full chewing power** — eat normally again
- ✅ **No toothless period** (immediate loading)
- ✅ **Bone loss is stopped** (implants transmit chewing forces)
- ✅ **Free palate** (upper jaw) — full taste
- ✅ **Natural aesthetics**, matched to your face
- ✅ **Long-lasting** — 20+ years with good care

## What about an "implant-supported denture"?

There's a middle path: the **overdenture** on 2-4 implants. It's removable but holds far better than a classic denture (via clips/bar) — and is cheaper than All-on-4. A good option on a limited budget or with certain medical conditions.

| Solution | Hold | Removable | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|
| Full denture | Low | Yes | Low |
| Overdenture (2-4 impl.) | Good | Yes | Medium |
| All-on-4 (fixed) | Very good | No | Higher |

## Cost over time

A full denture is cheaper but must be **replaced every 5-8 years** and doesn't stop bone loss. All-on-4 is a **one-off, long-term investment** (20+ years). Over 15-20 years the price gap narrows — with far greater comfort. [Cost overview →](/dental-treatment-cost-poland)

## Who's it for?

**All-on-4** makes sense if you want firm hold, full chewing function and a long-term solution that stops bone loss.

**Overdenture** as a compromise on a limited budget or if you prefer something removable.

**Classic full denture** as the cheapest entry option — with the known limitations.

## Conclusion

All-on-4 offers **firm hold, full chewing power and durability** — and stops the bone loss a denture can't. In Poland this high-quality solution is far more affordable than in the UK. Which option suits you, we'll determine after a CBCT.
