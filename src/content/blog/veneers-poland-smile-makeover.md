Discoloured, worn or slightly crooked front teeth can be corrected aesthetically with **veneers** (wafer-thin ceramic shells) — without heavily grinding down the teeth. In Poland, at a fraction of UK prices.

## What are veneers?

Veneers are **thin, light-translucent ceramic shells** bonded to the front of the front teeth. They correct:
- **discolouration** that can no longer be whitened
- **small gaps** between teeth
- **worn or chipped** edges
- **minor misalignment** (without braces)
- the **shape** of teeth that are too short or uneven

## Veneer vs crown — the difference

| | Veneer | Crown |
|---|---|---|
| Tissue removed | minimal (front only) | all around, more |
| Indication | aesthetic, intact teeth | heavily damaged teeth |
| Look | very natural | very natural |
| Durability | 10-15+ years | 10-15+ years |

For lightly affected teeth, a veneer is the **more tooth-preserving** choice.

## The process (usually 1-2 trips)

1. **Analysis & digital smile design** — you see the result in advance
2. **Minimal preparation** of the tooth's front surface
3. **Digital scan** (no unpleasant impression)
4. **Lab fabrication** of the ceramic shells
5. **Fitting & adjusting** — fine-tuning shape and colour

With digital planning the steps can be bundled efficiently into a few appointments.

## Durability & care

- **10-15 years and more** with good care
- normal oral hygiene, regular check-ups
- for grinders: a **night guard** to protect them

## Costs in Poland vs the UK

| | Poland | UK (approx.) |
|---|---|---|
| Veneer per tooth | €250-450 | £500-1,200 |
| Smile makeover (8-10 teeth) | €2,500-4,000 | £6,000-12,000 |

Especially for a **full smile makeover** (several front teeth) the saving is substantial. [Cost overview →](/dental-treatment-cost-poland)

## Natural, not "too perfect"

A good result does **not** look artificial. We match shape, length and colour to your face — harmonious, not a "Hollywood block". Digital smile design helps us agree the result together beforehand.

## Who it suits

- Patients with **healthy but aesthetically bothersome** front teeth
- Wanting a **even, brighter** smile
- As part of a **smile makeover** (often combined with cleaning/whitening)

## Conclusion

Veneers are the tooth-preserving route to a beautiful, natural smile — and far more affordable in Poland than in the UK, especially for a full makeover. Send us a photo of your smile for an initial assessment.
