The most common worry before treatment abroad is: **"What if something isn't right later — I'll be back home in the UK?"** A fair question. Here are honest answers about guarantee and aftercare.

## Is there a guarantee on dental work from Poland?

Yes. As in the UK, dental work in Poland carries a **guarantee**. At our clinic:
- **Implants:** long guarantee on the implant itself (brand manufacturers often give a lifetime manufacturer warranty)
- **Crowns, bridges, veneers:** multi-year guarantee on the prosthetic work
- The usual condition everywhere: **regular check-ups and good oral hygiene**

We set out the exact terms **in writing, in English**, before treatment begins — transparent, no surprises.

## What happens if there's a problem after you get home?

Three scenarios:

### 1. Something minor (e.g. a crown feels loose, a sharp edge)
Often a **local dentist** at home can adjust a small issue, usually at low cost. We'll advise you by phone/email on what to do.

### 2. Something on our work needs correcting
If it falls under the guarantee, **we correct it** — usually at an already-planned follow-up or a short extra visit. The treatment itself is then **free of charge** (you cover travel).

### 3. A genuine emergency
For acute pain/emergencies: **get it dealt with locally first**, then we agree the next steps together.

## Why most worries don't materialise

- **Modern diagnostics** (CBCT, microscope) lower the risk of complications significantly
- **Brand-name materials** instead of cheap products → long-lasting
- **Healing happens at home** — you're in the UK for exactly the period when most time passes
- Modern implantology has **success rates of 95-98%**

## How we plan to avoid problems

1. **Thorough planning upfront** (X-ray/CBCT, written treatment plan in English)
2. **Clean execution** with microscope and brand materials
3. **Clear care instructions** for home
4. **We stay reachable** — contact us in English
5. **Check-ups** sensibly combined with already-planned trips

## Practical tip: local dentist for routine check-ups

For your normal six-monthly check-up and hygiene visit you don't need to fly to Poland — your local dentist does that. You come to Opole only for the **actual treatment steps** and, if ever needed, a guarantee correction.

## Conclusion

Guarantee and aftercare are clearly defined: written guarantee, free correction in a guarantee case, routine care with your dentist at home. The "what if something happens" worry is understandable — but in practice well covered. Get in touch and we'll explain everything transparently.
